Introduction

vivichart is owned by IAW Limited, which is a privately owned company based in the United Kingdom. Whilst this service started in 2024, IAW's core business started over 35 years ago, and IAW has employed a number of its staff for 30+ years. IAW has had organisational modeling facilities within its existing software for over 20 years.

 

A description of the  vivichart service is available by clicking on this link  Overview

 

The service has been developed and is maintained in the English language. This English text appears to its users within

  • vivichart's web forms ("the Web Forms"),
  • messages that are sent to them by email ("the Messages"), and
  • the on-line 'help' documentation ("the On-Line Help").

 

IAW wishes to make the service available in as many, commercially-justifiable languages as possible in order to increase the size of its potential marketplace.

 The Requirement

The initial requirement is to have the Web Forms and the Messages translated.

The On-Line Help will be translated when the benefits of the English version are proven, based upon our clients' usage and their interaction with vivichart's support 'desk'.

 The Translation Process

The translation process for each item of text is

  1. the English language text is displayed to you
  2. you enter the foreign language equivalent
  3. you submit your translation for review (or wait to do so)
  4. a 'reviewer' accepts or rejects the item of text (normally when all items of text have been submitted for review), and
  5. the foreign language translations are released for live use within the vivichart service

 

A list of all the text that needs to be translated will be listed within a single form.

 

 

 

The four controls at the top of this list (Untranslated, Awaiting Review, etc.) allow you to show or restrict what appears within it.

 

Against each line of the English text (which appears in black), there is a Icon. If clicked upon, you will be provided with a comprehensive explanation of how the text is used within vivichart.

         

The foreign language text is displayed beneath each item of English text. Initially, it will displayed in blue. Once it has been viewed, it will be displayed in mauve.

 

The Icon will display a Dialog into which you can enter the foreign language text.

You can either

  • submit the translation for review,
  • save your translation (before, presumably, submitting it for review at a later time, when you have considered the translation further), or
  • cancel what you may have entered.

 

At the bottom of each form that shows context-related information, there is a "feedback" link that allows you to seek further clarification, if necessary. IAW will normally answer any query within the hour between 08:00 and 19:00 UK time.
