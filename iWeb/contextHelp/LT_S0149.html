Background

The Background is the area that is displayed either within a Chart or behind the Content Area of the Web Forms and is one of either:

  • an Image,
  • a Gradient or
  • a Single Colour.

(This word is also used in the context of the colour of the background of text, Tab Folders, $$Icons:Icon$, etc. In these contexts, it is always termed Background Colour).
