Viewing A Chart

A chart, which has been published, may be viewed by either

  • selecting it from a list, or
  • clicking on the link that you have been sent by email or otherwise have access to.

When viewing a chart, you have the following facilities available to affect its display, output it to a file and search its content for specific characters.

  

Click on the Collapsed or Expanded State of any box to see more or less information.
Left click on the blank chart area and drag the chart in any direction to move its content.

Enter the information that you want to find and click the Search icon. All boxes, which contain the characters that you have entered, will be highlighted.

The    icon will remove the highlights.

Use either to change the size of the content within the chart. The mouse wheel will have a similar affect.
Click upon the magnifier icon and a rectangular area will appear within the chart. This rectangle can be repositioned by using the mouse in order to view the chart's content in greater detail. The magnified area can be hidden by clicking anywhere within the chart. (This icon is displayed only when the chart is reduced to 70% or more of its original size).
Click this Information button to see a Note, if one has been entered for any box within the Chart.
Provided that you have been granted permission, these buttons allow you to see earlier and later versions of the current chart.
Click on the PDF icon to download the chart as a PDF file.
This icon will appear within a box if further information is held. Click on the icon to view it.
Right click on the blank chart area and select either Zoom to Fit to see the entire chart or Centre at Top to enlarge the content and position the top level box in the centre, and at the top of the screen.
