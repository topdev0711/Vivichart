Populating A Chart

Overview

 

A chart is populated from the Selection List  by dragging and dropping a row at a time into the chart area in order to add Nodes and establish reporting relationships.

 

Whilst vivichart will position information within the chart itself, based upon the reporting relationships that a Chart Builder has defined, Nodes may be moved to the right and left of their siblings.

 

Charts are built from the top, down. The first Node to be added should be that which is to appear at the top and may be one of either a Data Node, a Group Node or a  Co-Parent Node.
